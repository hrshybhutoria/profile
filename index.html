Cultural Heritage

After 14 years of Bharatanatyam training, I've danced everywhere from art museums and libraries to city-wide festivals, telling ancient stories through precise gestures and rhythm. Every time I dance, I'm opening a window into a part of who I am and inviting others in.

Family Legacy

Service has always been constant in my life. I would go with my grandfather to shelters and orphanages, handing out food and blankets. His legacy lives on through the people who received his donated corneas after he passed, and through our family's nonprofit.
